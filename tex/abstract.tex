\textbf{%
Kuma, P., 2010: Visualising Data from CloudSat and CALIPSO Satellites.
Comenius University, Bratislava, Faculty of Mathematics, Physics and
Informatics,
Department of Astronomy, Physics of the Earth and Meteorology. 77pp. Mentor:
Bašták Ďurán, I.}\\

\noindent CloudSat and CALIPSO are NASA, resp. joint NASA and CNES polar-orbiting Earth
observation satellites. CloudSat carries a millimetre-wave radar for observation
of clouds. CALIPSO carries a visible and infrared polarisation-sensitive lidar for
observation of aerosols and ice-phase clouds. Data from these satellites are
distributed
in the form of HDF4 and \mbox{HDF-EOS2} product files. We introduce a software tool
\textit{ccplot} capable of visualising several data sets from the \textit{CloudSat 2B-GEOPROF},
\textit{CALIPSO Lidar L1B Profiles}, \textit{CALIPSO Lidar L2 Cloud Layer}
and \textit{Aqua MODIS L1B} products. \textit{ccplot} is a scriptable, unix command-line tool.
We released \textit{ccplot} on the Internet under the open-source-compatible BSD license.\\

\noindent \textbf{Keywords:} CloudSat, CALIPSO, MODIS, A-Train, HDF4, HDF-EOS2, ccplot, plot,
visualization
